Designed to organize the purchases and sales of investments in a portfolio, showing a lot of wallet results.

It is not recommended for DAY TRADER, it will be better used for BUY AND HOLDER.

With MyWallet you will allow for:

  1. Create investment types and organize them into types and portfolio weights
  2. Create brokers to associate the actions
  3. Register "BUY" and "SELL" investments
  4. Register new transactions for your investments
  5. The investment table can be filtered by investiment type
  6. The investments table will show:
    • Real-time investment quotes
    • Current amount in the portfolio, in green for buy positions and in red for sell positions
    • The amount paid on the acquisition of the investment
    • The current value of the investment in the portfolioThe current value of the investment in the portfolio
    • The updated profit in %
    • The weight in % of each investment in the portfolio, and how much was planned
    • How much is left to buy what was planned for the stock
    • The related broker
    • If the position is zeroed, the line will turn into gray and it will show the realized profit in value and percentage
  7. The transaction's table can be filtered by investment
  8. Create goals and measure how much is left to reach your ideal equity
